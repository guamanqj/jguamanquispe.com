## From Data Foundations to AI Innovation

I am a **Data Engineer** with over 11+ years of experience building the robust architectures that power large-scale enterprise decisions. My journey began at **Verizon**, where I progressed from developing ETL pipelines to architecting Business Intelligence solutions for complex telecommunications data.

Throughout my career, I’ve specialized in the "plumbing" of data—ensuring that information is clean, scalable, and accessible. I’ve spent years mastering **ETL (Extract, Transform, and Load) Process**, performing complex **SQL optimization**, and **Data Warehousing (Fact/Dimension modeling)**. If there is a bottleneck in a pipeline or a query that needs tuning, I’ve likely solved a similar version of it.

### Why Data Science?
Engineering the data is only half the story. My passion lies in what happens *after* the pipeline ends. I am currently transitioning into **Data Science and AI**, leveraging my deep engineering roots to build models that aren't just accurate, but production-ready. 

I’ve recently augmented my 11+ years of hands-on experience with:
* **A Master of Science in Computer Science from Stevens Institute of Technology - 2024**
* **A Certificate in Business Intelligence from Stevens Institute of Technology - 2024**
* **A Certificate in Data Science from Harvard Extension School - 2019**

### What I Bring to the Table
I don't just build models in a vacuum. I understand the underlying infrastructure required to make AI work in the real world. Whether it’s containerizing an application with **Docker** or designing a schema that supports real-time analytics, I focus on the intersection of **Software Engineering**, **Data Architecture**, and **Machine Learning**.
